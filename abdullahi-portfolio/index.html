About the Author

Hello, I am Abdullahi Ibrahim. I am an author of 11+ books, a dedicated kindergarten teacher, and a storyteller passionate about motivation, personal growth, and the immigrant journey.

My writing spans across multiple genres including mystery, crime, personal finance, diaspora stories, children’s literature, and emotional wellness. Beyond the page, I create motivational content, run HarawoTalks, and engage in entrepreneurship projects.

"My mission is simple: helping people rise, heal, learn, and re-imagine their future through the power of stories and shared knowledge."

The Courage to Start Over

Starting over is never easy. It asks for a kind of bravery that happens in silence — in early mornings, long nights, and moments when no one sees you. Most people only see the result, not the private battles that lead to the first step forward.

You do not start from zero; you start from experience. Experience makes you wiser, stronger, and more aware of who you are and what you deserve. Every time you choose to rise again, you are practicing courage, even if nobody claps for you.

Why I Write Mystery Fiction

Mystery fiction is more than suspense; it is a mirror into human behavior. When characters are under pressure, their true nature rises to the surface — fear, loyalty, guilt, love, and regret all show up at once.

Through mystery, I explore questions about truth, silence, justice, and consequence. Behind every secret or crime, there is a human heart trying to protect something. That tension between what is hidden and what is revealed is why I am drawn to this genre.
